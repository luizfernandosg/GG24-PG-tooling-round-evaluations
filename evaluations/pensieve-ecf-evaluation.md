## Key Aspects

### Integration/Development in Question
Iteration of Pensieve.ECF platform: (1) Iterate Elderwand Evaluation Engine Alpha with new evaluation items and metrics (e.g., Participation Rank); (2) Integrate Elderwand with KarmaGAP Framework enabling shared evaluation standards and interoperability. Addresses information fragmentation and verification challenges in public goods evaluation.

### Funding Amount
$15,000 requested ($6,000 research/design, $7,000 engineering/development, $2,000 verification/documentation)

### General Impression
Ambitious and innovative proposal addressing collective sense-making challenge. Strong research foundation with whitepaper and Elderwand Evaluation Engine. Has active alpha with 100+ projects. However, budget ($15,000) is high and timeline not explicitly confirmed. Research-focused delivery may be less tangible than code deliverables.

### Alignment with Round
- **Interoperability:** Strong - integrates KarmaGAP, Aragon OSx, Sablier, Agora, mentions Arweave for data storage
- **Collaboration:** Good - KarmaGAP integration, Perennial Grant Campaign, mentions Gitcoin, Octant collaborations
- **UI/UX:** Good - addresses information fragmentation and collective sense-making UX challenge
- **Product Maturity:** Good - has active alpha with 100+ projects, realistic scope for iteration

### UX Impact (20 points)
**Focus:** Clear definition of the UX problem (onboarding, account abstraction, safety, gas, dev-ex, privacy, etc.), severity evidence, and test plan.
