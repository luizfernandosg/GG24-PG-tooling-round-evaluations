# Application Form — Alignment Map (Sections 2, 6, 7)

This map shows where key program elements appear to ensure consistency across sections.

- Two-phase structure
  - Primary: Section 2 (overview and participant commitments)
  - Cross-references: Section 6 (funding triggers) and Section 7 (evaluation lens)

- Funding envelope and matching
  - Primary: Section 6 (totals, 2x matching, max €20k, ops 20%)
  - Cross-reference: Section 2 (context) and Section 7 (implications for Phase 2)

- Operations pool (20%) and governance
  - Primary: Section 6 (purpose, uses)
  - Cross-reference: Section 2 (Safe MultiSig governance overview)

- Baseline distribution (€1,000 per project)
  - Primary: Section 6 (eligibility and timing after Phase 1)
  - Cross-reference: Section 2 (Phase 1 unlocks) and Section 7 (not outcome-contingent)

- Additional distribution (Phase 2)
  - Primary: Section 6 (availability and cadence)
  - Cross-reference: Section 7 (qualitative criteria guiding allocation)

- Evaluation approach and criteria
  - Primary: Section 7 (qualitative, process- and learning-focused)
  - Cross-reference: Section 2 (participant documentation commitments)

- Fragile contexts and risk tolerance
  - Primary: Section 7 (special consideration and acceptance of failure)
  - Cross-reference: Section 2 (program values)

- Timelines and milestones
  - Section 2: Phase 1 before Christmas; workshop participation
  - Section 6: local fund transfers before end of month; disbursement cadence
  - Section 7: Phase 1 verification timing; rolling Phase 2 assessments

- Disbursement mechanics (Safe MultiSig)
  - Primary: Section 6 (treasury and approvals)
  - Cross-reference: Section 2 (governance overview)
