## üîπ **UX Impact**

### **UX Impact Score: 15/20**

### **Rationale:**

The UX problem is clearly defined: the pseudonymous nature of blockchain networks makes it challenging to identify and empower key contributors, leading to most programs prioritizing low-value contributions (simple app clicks) over long-term, high-value contributions that drive real value and development. User Quality Score enables better prioritization by labeling onchain transactions to account levels.

Severity and evidence are strong - the narrative clearly articulates how current systems favor clicks over meaningful contributions, creating misaligned incentives. The proposal mentions that User Quality Score will improve efficiency of onchain growth programs and function as incentive to promote wider Celo dApp usage and sign of quality for emerging apps.

The test/validation plan mentions Divvi integration powering Proof of Impact program (with remaining budget of over $100K in CELO and OP tokens) but doesn't specify success metrics or validation approach. The proposal should define KPIs such as API users, quality scores generated, impact on growth programs, or improvement in contribution quality.

Impact potential is high - improves ecosystem growth program efficiency, enables better user prioritization, and incentivizes wider dApp usage. Overall assessment is moderate to strong - clear problem with strong narrative, but validation plan needs more specificity.
