## üîπ **UX Impact**

### **UX Impact Score: 18/20**

### **Rationale:**

The UX problem is clearly defined: loss of digital sovereignty due to centralized infrastructure dependency (PGF coordination and history tethered to AWS, Discord), creating single point of failure for censorship and data loss. Additionally, voter coercion threat in governance undermines honest coordination. The proposal addresses both through decentralized social data layer and ZKP integration for anonymous voting.

Severity and evidence are strong - the narrative clearly articulates how centralized infrastructure creates single point of failure and how voter coercion undermines honest coordination. The proposal mentions targeting specialized groups (Decentralized Space Startups, Genome Researchers, Agricultural Cooperatives) that suffer most from centralized platform risk.

The test/validation plan mentions specialized groups but doesn't specify success metrics or validation approach. The proposal should define KPIs such as users onboarded, posts created, tipping volume, ZKP usage, and governance participation metrics. Impact potential is high - addresses fundamental governance and sovereignty challenges if successful.

Overall assessment is strong - clear problem definition with strong narrative, though validation plan needs more specificity.
